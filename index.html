Project Description & Research

Arcane Runner merges fast movement with projectile-heavy boss fights and careful map design. The project emphasizes:

  • Player feel: Precise acceleration, dash tuning, and stop/start movement.
  • Bullet patterns: Repeating waves, rotating bursts, and destroyable projectile clouds.
  • Modular demos: Small focused builds to validate ideas quickly (laser, sprites, moves, full game).
  • Iteration log: Daily entries record challenges and solutions for judges and collaborators.

Research & Resources

  1. Bullet-hell pattern design articles and math references.
  2. Tutorials on collision systems and fixed timestep physics.
  3. Performance tips for large numbers of projectiles.
