Project Description & Unique Value

Arcane Runner focuses on merging the clarity of action-RPG controls with the spectacle of bullet-hell boss design. The aim is to deliver encounters that reward spatial awareness and timing rather than memorization.

Core systems

  • Deterministic player movement
  • Projectile and collision subsystem
  • Boss quadrant and phase manager
  • Destroyable projectile waves (player can clear bullets)

Why this solves a problem

By modularizing boss moves and decoupling the projectile engine from visuals, rapid iteration is possible without deep engine changes. This lets designers experiment with telegraphs and counterplay quickly.

Development — Research & Daily Logs

Implemented Features (detailed)

  • Map collision + nav: tile-based collision with edge correction
  • Real-time combat: frame-based hit detection and sfx management
  • Projectile engine: pooled projectiles, destroyable waves, and collision masks
  • Dash & sprint: velocity-based dash with cooldown and invulnerability frames
  • Boss AI: quadrant movement, state transitions, spawn handlers
